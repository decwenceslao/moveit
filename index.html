EXPERIENCE GRAPEVINE TX

Facts to Know About Grapevine & DFW Area

Moving is no doubt a stressful period in anyone’s life. Whether you’re moving your home or your office in Grapevine TX, there are many questions you may have about the process. How much does it all cost, which city is the best fit for you, and which neighborhood you should settle in? We get a lot of these questions here at Grapevine Prime Movers, so here are some answers to those common questions.

Is it expensive to live in Grapevine?

The cost of living in Grapevine TX is slightly higher than the state average, but it's still less than many big cities.

Grapevine has a high standard of living, and its surrounding areas offer ample amenities and attractions that more than cover the extra few dollars you would spend on rent or groceries every month. The DFW Metroplex offers plenty of affordable Grapevine TX apartments for rent and nice neighborhoods in which to live.

What is the best neighborhood in Grapevine?

Grapevine has a variety of amazing neighborhoods, each with its own feel and special attractions. To decide which neighborhood suits you best, it's a good idea to do your research before you move. There are many websites online where you can read reviews, look at pictures of the area, and learn about local life.

What is there to do in Grapevine?

Grapevine enjoys a rich culture, highlighted by art galleries, concerts, festivals, sports events, family activities and more. The city has several shopping centers with restaurants, stores, and other fun activities. There are also many parks, lakes, running trails, campgrounds, golf courses and more to enjoy throughout the Grapevine-Colleyville area.

Is it safe to live in Grapevine?

Grapevine is one of the safest cities around, with safe neighborhoods and low crime rates. The Grapevine Police Department is well known for its exemplary police work, and the Grapevine Fire Department works hard to keep everyone safe. Many people from other parts of Texas move to Grapevine TX because they want a safe place far away from high crime rates. The DFW area in general has some of the lowest crime rates around, so you can relax and be safe in Grapevine.

Is it worth living in Grapevine?

If you're looking for a family-friendly city with safe streets, affordable housing and low crime rates, Grapevine is definitely worth considering. You can live in Grapevine's fantastic neighborhoods, attend one of the many great schools or visit the fun attractions around town. No matter what you're looking for in a long term home, you'll be able to find it in Grapevine.

What are the best places to live in Grapevine ?

If you're looking for a safe place to settle down with your family, fantastic schools and lots of great amenities, there are several neighborhoods that should be considered. The top neighborhoods in Grapevine include: 

- Cross Timbers: This quiet community has beautiful homes with limited access, easy convenience to Hwy 121 and near major shopping centers. 

- Park Place Estates: This community provides outstanding amenities, privacy and is convenient to Grapevine Lake. 

- Country Club Manor: If you enjoy low-maintenance living with the comfort of an established neighborhood, this is a great place for you.

What are the best schools in Grapevine?

Grapevine is home to several great public and private schools, including Regis Catholic School, Holy Spirit Preparatory School, Christa McAuliffe Academy of International Studies, Grapevine Faith Christian Academy, Grapevine High School and more. 

For higher education needs you can attend one of the many colleges near Grapevine TX, including Texas Christian University, Dallas Baptist University, Northwood University - Texas Campus, Brookhaven College, Richland College and more.

What are the most affordable cities in DFW area?

If you're looking for affordable housing or an inexpensive place to rent an apartment or house in the DFW area, there are plenty of great options. Cities near Grapevine that offer some of the most affordable rent include Burleson, Crowley, Kennedale, Mansfield and Rio Vista. For homeowners looking to save money on their home loan, cities like Waxahachie, Burleson and Cleburne have some of the lowest rates in the DFW area.

How far is Grapevine from Dallas?

Dallas and Grapevine are only 15 minutes apart by car, so you'll be at your destination in no time. You can also take public transportation or hop on another city's metro to reach your destination faster. If you want to visit other cities for work or pleasure, you'll find that the DFW Metroplex offers many great opportunities for entertainment and business.

What is there to do in Dallas?

There's always something exciting going on in Dallas. From the Dallas World Aquarium to the Nasher Sculpture Center to the JFK Memorial Plaza, there are lots of fun things for everyone to enjoy. If you're looking for a night out on the town, there are lots of great bars and restaurants to choose from. The Dallas Zoo, Six Flags Over Texas and the Fort Worth Stockyards all offer great family fun. There is something to do for everyone in Dallas.

What is Grapevine TX known for?

The city of Grapevine TX is known for being the "Christmas Capital of Texas." The Tree Lighting Ceremony is held every year on Thanksgiving Day at North Lake Park.

Grapevine wasn't always known as the Christmas Capital, but has since become synonymous with holiday traditions. This tradition drives shoppers to the area during the busiest season of the year, and keeps them coming back every November to see the spectacular holiday lights display.
